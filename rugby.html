New Zealand "All Blacks"

  • Major Achievements:
    1. World Cup Champions (2011)
    2. World Cup Champions (2015)
    3. Won the Rugby Championship multiple times
    4. Consistently ranked #1 in the world

Japan "Brave Blossoms"

  • Major Achievements:
    1. First Rugby World Cup Win (2015 vs. South Africa)
    2. Hosted Rugby World Cup (2019)
    3. Developed a strong competitive team in Asia
    4. Consistently improving world ranking

South Africa "Springboks"

  • Major Achievements:
    1. World Cup Champions (1995)
    2. World Cup Champions (2007)
    3. World Cup Champions (2019)
    4. World Cup Champions (2023)
    5. Has been the #1 ranked team

France "Les Bleus"

  • Major Achievements:
    1. Six Nations Champions (multiple times)
    2. Runners-up in Rugby World Cup (1987, 1999, 2011, 2023)
    3. Has been a top-ranked European team

England "Red Roses"

  • Major Achievements:
    1. Rugby World Cup Winners (2003)
    2. Six Nations Champions (multiple times)
    3. Multiple Six Nations Grand Slams
    4. Consistently ranked among top teams in the world
